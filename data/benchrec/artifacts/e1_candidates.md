- **Implication:** the headline auto-match number is coupled to N:M, exactly as the review warned - a 1:1 rule alone cannot reach 99.8% *auto* on the full population. E2 (character features) serves the narrower job of disambiguating genuinely ambiguous 1:1 blocks.
